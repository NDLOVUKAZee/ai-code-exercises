Exercise part 3
1. The main entities are Task, TaskStatus, TaskPriority, TaskManager, and TaskStorage.

2. A Task represents a piece of work that contains a title, description, priority, status, due date, and tags.

3. TaskStatus represents the current state of a task.

4. TaskPriority represents the importance level of a task.

5. A task is considered overdue when its due date has passed and it is not marked as DONE.

6. TaskManager handles task operations such as creating, updating, and deleting tasks.

7. TaskStorage is responsible for storing and retrieving tasks.

Questions:

1. What status values are available besides TODO and DONE?

2. What priority levels are available?

Glossary:

Task - A piece of work to be completed.

Status - The current state of a task.

Priority - The importance level of a task.

Due Date - The deadline for completing a task.

Tag - A label used to organise tasks.

Overdue Task - A task that has passed its due date and is not completed.

That's probably all you need unless your lecturer specifically asked for detailed answers.
